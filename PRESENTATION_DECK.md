## 🎮 Live Demo Flow

### Demo Scenario: Apple Inc. (AAPL) Analysis

#### Phase 1: Research (GenAI Module) - 2 minutes
1. **Select Stock**: Enter "AAPL" and date range (last 30 days)
2. **News Analysis**: 
   - Show fetched articles with relevance scores
   - Display sentiment classification (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral)
   - Demonstrate filtering by relevance threshold
3. **AI Briefing**: 
   - Generate market briefing using Gemini AI
   - Show structured output: sentiment, catalysts, risks
4. **Press Release**: Upload/process latest quarterly report PDF

#### Phase 2: Technical Analysis (Charting Module) - 3 minutes
1. **Chart Visualization**:
   - Load AAPL real-time candlestick chart
   - Demonstrate zoom, pan, crosshair functionality
   - Switch between timeframes (1D, 1W, 1M)
2. **Technical Indicators**:
   - Apply SMA, RSI, MACD indicators
   - Show customizable parameters
   - Demonstrate indicator overlay display
3. **Trade Setups**:
   - Identify support/resistance levels
   - Show trade setup recommendations
   - Configure proximity alerts (3% threshold)

#### Phase 3: Trading Execution (Trading Module) - 2 minutes
1. **Order Placement**:
   - Place market order based on analysis
   - Demonstrate limit order with stop-loss
   - Show order book and real-time updates
2. **Portfolio Tracking**:
   - View updated portfolio with P&L
   - Show performance metrics and charts
   - Demonstrate risk assessment tools

### Expected Demo Outcomes
- **Research Time**: 30 seconds vs 30 minutes manual
- **Technical Analysis**: Professional-grade insights instantly
- **Trade Execution**: Realistic simulation with immediate feedback
- **Integrated Workflow**: Seamless transition between phases

## 🎤 Panel Q&A Preparation

### Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: How does your AI analysis compare to human financial analysts?**
A: Our AI provides speed and consistency - analyzing hundreds of articles in seconds vs hours for humans. It complements rather than replaces human judgment, providing the initial analysis that analysts can build upon.

**Q: What's your strategy for handling market data costs?**
A: We use a tiered approach: delayed data for free users, real-time for premium. We've negotiated volume discounts and implement intelligent caching to minimize API calls while maintaining data quality.

**Q: How do you ensure the accuracy of sentiment analysis?**
A: We use a multi-source approach with relevance weighting, validate against market movements, and continuously train our models. Current accuracy is 87% with ongoing improvements.

**Q: What's your competitive moat against established platforms?**
A: Integration is our key differentiator. While others offer individual components, we provide a complete workflow from research to execution. Our AI-powered analysis and educational focus also set us apart.

**Q: How will you scale user acquisition?**
A: Freemium model for organic growth, partnerships with trading education platforms, content marketing through trading communities, and referral programs for existing users.
