## 📊 **Technical Indicators Explained**

### **Why These 5 Indicators?**
We selected these indicators because they provide **comprehensive market analysis** covering:
- **Trend Direction** (SMA, EMA)
- **Momentum** (RSI, MACD) 
- **Volume Analysis** (VWAP)

### **1. 📈 Simple Moving Average (SMA)**
- **Purpose**: Identifies trend direction and support/resistance levels
- **How it works**: Average of closing prices over a specific period
- **Trading Use**: Price above SMA = uptrend, below = downtrend

### **2. 📊 Exponential Moving Average (EMA)**
- **Purpose**: More responsive to recent price changes than SMA
- **How it works**: Gives more weight to recent prices
- **Trading Use**: Faster signal generation, better for short-term trading

### **3. ⚡ Relative Strength Index (RSI)**
- **Purpose**: Measures momentum and identifies overbought/oversold conditions
- **Range**: 0-100 (>70 = overbought, <30 = oversold)
- **Trading Use**: Entry/exit signals when market reversal is likely

### **4. 🎯 MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)**
- **Purpose**: Identifies trend changes and momentum shifts
- **Components**: MACD line, Signal line, Histogram
- **Trading Use**: Buy when MACD crosses above signal line, sell when below

### **5. 💰 VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)**
- **Purpose**: Shows average price weighted by trading volume
- **How it works**: Considers both price and volume for true market value
- **Trading Use**: Institutional benchmark - price above VWAP indicates strength
