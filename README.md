## 🎯 Project Overview

This platform integrates three powerful components to create a complete trading workflow:

1. **🤖 GenAI Sentiment Analysis** - AI-powered news analysis with Google Gemini integration
2. **📈 Professional Charting** - TradingView-style interface with technical indicators
3. **💼 Virtual Trading Platform** - Risk-free trading simulation with portfolio management

- ✅ **90% time savings** in market research through AI automation
- ✅ **Professional-grade tools** accessible to retail traders
- ✅ **Seamless integration** across research, analysis, and execution
- ✅ **Real-time performance** with sub-second response times
